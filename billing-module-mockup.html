Arch How Billing Connects to Onboarding — for the team
1
Onboarding Portal
Customer fills form, signs agreement, makes first payment. Sales confirms → status becomes ops_setup.
2
Ops Activates Account
Ops provisions client in c-dash, links Razorpay customer token, sets plan limits, marks → active.
3
c-dash Access
Client gets login credentials. Billing tab shows from day 1 — first renewal already scheduled in Razorpay.
4
Recurring Billing
Razorpay auto-charges each cycle. Webhook creates invoice record, sends email receipt. Failed → retry + alert.
Phase 1 — Build First
  • Billing Overview screen
  • Invoice list with PDF download
  • Payment failed alert + retry button
  • Saved payment methods list
  • Razorpay webhook → invoice records in DB
  • Auto-pay on/off toggle
Phase 2 — Later
  • Self-serve plan upgrade / downgrade
  • Usage alerts at 90% threshold
  • Add / change payment method in-app
  • Annual billing with discount
  • GST invoice with GSTIN auto-fill
Key decision: Use Razorpay Subscriptions (UPI mandate) for recurring billing — customer saves mandate once, gets auto-charged every month/quarter. No manual intervention needed. Offline clients continue as-is via sales portal manual confirmation. Both flows already handled by the current onboarding portal.
